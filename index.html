Creative co-working space

A co-working space for creatives. This is not just a "desk for hire" on a daily, weekly or monthly basis. This is a space created for the photographer or graphic designer (or even accountant!) working from home who needs a professional space to get out of the home office.

Co-working convenience

Whether it's a large project you're working on and you need the space to concentrate, a promo photo you need to take, a meeting with an important client, or just to be around other creatives – this space is designed for you.
