If you are searching for a photo studio in Sydney, where you shoot matters almost as much as the space itself. The right location is easy to reach, surrounded by character, and close to everything you might need on the day.

At Desk and Studio, we are proud to call Sydney's Inner West home. Our studio sits in Petersham, just across from Norton Street in Leichhardt, in one of the most creative pockets of the city. Here is why the Inner West is such a lovely place to shoot, and what you can expect when you hire our space.

## Why the Inner West is a great place to shoot

The Inner West has long been a magnet for makers, photographers and small creative businesses, and it is easy to see why. It is central without the rush of the CBD, it is full of texture and personality, and it is genuinely easy to get to from across Sydney.

For a shoot, that combination is hard to beat. You can load in without battling city traffic, grab a coffee around the corner between setups, and step into a space that feels relaxed and inspiring rather than corporate. Stepping into a studio should make you feel ready to create, and the neighbourhood plays its part in that.

## Where we are

You will find us in **Petersham**, in Sydney's Inner West, a short hop from **Leichhardt**, **Marrickville**, **Stanmore**, **Newtown** and **Annandale**. We are across from the well-loved Norton Street precinct, so you are never far from a good coffee or a bite to eat.

For anyone coming from further afield, the Inner West is well connected by train and bus, and it is an easy drive from the CBD, the Eastern Suburbs and the Inner South. If you are travelling with gear, it is a far gentler trip than heading into the heart of the city.

The area is also a genuine creative hub, full of galleries, cafes, makers and small studios, which means you are shooting somewhere that already hums with the kind of energy creative work thrives on. It is the sort of place where you can find a backdrop on the street, a coffee in a minute, and a quiet corner to review your shots, all within a short walk of the studio door.

## Getting here and planning your visit

A little planning makes your shoot day run smoothly. Here are a few things worth sorting before you arrive:

1. **Plan your route.** We are in Petersham, close to public transport and an easy drive from most of Sydney. Allow a few extra minutes if you are carrying gear.
2. **Think about loading in.** If you are bringing a lot of equipment, plan how you will move it from the car to the studio so the first part of your booking goes into shooting, not lugging.
3. **Book the time you need.** A half-day or full day gives you room to work through several setups without rushing. You can always plan your shot list in advance to make the most of it.
4. **Ask us anything beforehand.** If you are unsure what is included or how to set up a particular look, send us a message before the day. We are always happy to help.
